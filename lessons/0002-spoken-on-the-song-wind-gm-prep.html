The one-sentence pitch

Two weeks after Book 1, **Janatimo** mentors the PCs through open-ended investigations that expose **serpentfolk spies** hunting Koride’s **Vesicant Egg** and a **grippli crime boss** who weaponized griffons — then the party becomes **conversants**, runs a **sandbox of city missions**, storms **Mayor Asanda’s flooded manor**, and **graduates as lore-speakers** (teachers) at level 8.

Chapter 1 — beat by beat

Janatimo’s audience (~2 weeks post–Book 1)

  • Uzunjati Learned One returns; summons PCs to his office (canal-side building).
  • Debrief Book 1: anadi arrival, griffon attack, Stone Ghost — he wants theories, not answers.
  • Four debate questions (realistic vs outlandish answers; skill checks → Impression Points). 80–120 XP.
  • Gift: crown of the companion to aid investigations.

Investigation A — Anadi students (Hababe Building)

  • Find Goss, Mazta, Savana via Tzeniwe (human-form peer; knows the newcomers) or Gather Information (Diplomacy DC 17).
  • They still prefer hybrid/spider forms at Hababe — unsettles classmates. Contrast Tzeniwe, who usually passes as human and softens reactions with gifts/snacks.
  • Anadi debate fate/causality; wary of animated clay guardian statues (Salathiss disabled them, re-boarded secret door).
  • Night stakeout → serpentfolk activity; secret storeroom with **Coil spies**, traps, snakes guarding approach to Koride’s egg lab.
  • Clue: Salathiss studies **Vesicant Egg** via passwall into Koride’s hidden lab (PCs can’t follow that route).

Investigation B — Griffon source (Oba Eze)

  • Oba Eze — animal vendor; sold griffons; suspects **Froglegs** (grippli crime boss) released them.
  • Shop may be under attack by Froglegs’s bandits (meat tent sabotage, cage break-ins).
  • Sets up Ch 2 “Finding Froglegs” and animal-welfare themes.

Chapter 2 — conversant sandbox

Promotion ceremony

Janatimo advances PCs to conversant — represent Magaambya in Nantambu while still studying. Each PC speaks on “best use of magic in the world.”

Chapter 3 — flooded manor & graduation

Salathiss (serpentfolk) posed as laborers at Asanda’s manor, weakened walls, breached a canal, flooded the building, kidnapped **Asanda**, and impersonates him to infiltrate city government. Goal: steal Magaambyan secrets (egg); now broader coup.

Janatimo sends PCs in with scrolls (suggests clairvoyance, freedom of movement, restoration). Dungeon crawl through flooded manor + serpentfolk guards.

Graduation — lore-speakers

  • After saving Asanda and completing coursework, PCs **graduate** — become **lore-speakers** (teachers).
  • Janatimo asks probationary teaching stay (AP assumes PCs teach from Book 3 on).
  • Ceremony at the **name obelisk**; each PC gives a speech on what magic still has to teach them.
  • Nantambu contacts (Tokku, Amaechi, etc.) send congratulations.

Threads to Book 3+

  • Serpentfolk want the **Vesicant Egg** — Koride still hiding it; Salathiss can’t reach lab directly.
  • Insect swarms continue — egg’s pull never stopped.
  • PCs are **teachers** next — Book 3 (*Hurricane's Howl*) sends them with student cohort to Sodden Lands.
  • Froglegs may escape custody — recurring nuisance if you want continuity.

GM tips

  • Don’t run all 10 missions if pacing drags — pick missions that match PC interests and foreshadow Asanda.
  • Keep Spire Dorm alive — Book 2 has less dorm content; interleave slice-of-life between tasks.
  • Reward theories — Janatimo’s Impression Point scene sets tone for whole book.
  • Graduation is a milestone — let speeches land; whole campus + city witnesses.
