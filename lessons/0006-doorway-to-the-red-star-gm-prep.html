The one-sentence pitch

After **Dimari-Diji** points them at **Old-Mage Jatembe** on Akiton, PCs teach one more **3-month semester**, research the **Doorway to the Red Star**, earn passage from the **Iobane** guardians, aid **Contemplatives of Ashok**, race across the desert in **sand racers**, board the **Mandibles of Fate**, free **Jatembe**, and defeat the **King of Biting Ants** — triggering his contingency that awakens Book 6's egg.

Chapter 1 — semester & doorway

  • Portal opens only when Golarion & Akiton align — window lands **after** upcoming semester (convenient pacing).
  • Run semester in **2-week increments** + one faculty event per block (players pick which mentor to visit).
  • Preparatory Research topics (Iobane, King, Akiton geography, etc.) — NPCs suggest if PCs stall.
  • Branch advancement still needs **Practical Research** in field — expedition is the lever.
  • Doorway guarded by **Iobane** (Zenj magi order, Jatembe allies) vs. **Throat-of-Nothingness** undead remnants.
  • Clear undead → earn right to use portal. Optional: **Spellcutter** or Jatembe-linked gear as diplomatic gifts.

Chapters 2–3 — Akiton & airship

  • Contemplatives of Ashok — brain-creature scholars; defend Hall of Reason before desert travel.
  • Seldo — acquire sand racers / boarding tech; social encounter with feuding criminals.
  • Open desert chase — high-level set-piece; skip or trim random encounters if chase drags.
  • Mandibles of Fate — King's airship; Jatembe imprisoned aboard.
  • Finale: free Jatembe, defeat King (banished again, not necessarily destroyed). **His contingency hatches the Vesicant Egg** — Book 6 cold open.
Book 6 bridge: PCs return at 18th with Jatembe. Magic Warrior ceremony → discover Koride & cohort Unshadowed. Don't spoil egg details here; let Book 5 ending feel like victory.

GM tips

  • Ch 1 downtime: don't roll endlessly — pick 3–4 faculty events that matter to your table.
  • Research Points — track loosely; success = better intel on Akiton, not gatekeeping fun.
  • Interplanetary travel: module notes 9th-level teleport edge case — 15th-level party shouldn't trivialize; doorway is the intended route.
  • Pacing: semester + Akiton + chase + airship is a lot; trim Seldo side content if needed.

Into Book 6

  • King's defeat → **Vesicant Egg** awakens on campus.
  • Jatembe present but **defers** during Magic Warrior ceremony and Circle ritual.
  • Koride & Spire Dorm friends — Unshadowed — pay off Books 1–2 cohort investment.
