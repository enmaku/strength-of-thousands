The one-sentence pitch

Famous lore-speakers join a **Mzali diplomatic delegation** to soften **Walkena's** isolationism — earn audience with the undead god-king, survive the **Shrine of the Eclipse**, discover **Osibu** (lost city of gold), then **defend Dimari-Diji's ritual** when Walkena's army marches to plunder it — ending at **15th level** with Jatembe's trail pointing to Akiton.

Chapter 1 — diplomacy, not revolution

Paizo sidebar "Dealing with Evil": Magaambya doesn't assassinate Walkena or liberate Mzali by coup. Goal = incremental openness — trade, visitors, fewer draconian killings. Redemption through intentional change, not a single boss fight in the throne room.
  • Oyamba summons PCs to Archhorn Library with faculty (Takulu Ot, Koride, others).
  • Walkena — child sun-king returned from death ~century ago; freed Mzali from Chelaxians, replaced one tyranny with another.
  • Council of Mwanyisa — inner circle PCs must win over, one favor at a time.
  • Weeks of negotiation possible — run as influence/social sandbox; each official has hooks.
  • Success metric: audience with Walkena + policy shift, not his destruction.

Chapters 2–3 — shrine & siege

  • Shrine task is Walkena's "final test" before negotiations complete.
  • Dajermube (sun goddess of eclipses) — temple cleansing = level 14 milestone.
  • Osibu revelation: hidden golden city; stewardship vs. plunder.
  • Dimari-Diji — ancient arboreal defender; ritual buys time against Walkena's forces.
  • Dimari-Diji tells PCs **Jatembe is on Akiton** — sets up Book 5 (even if PCs already wanted to go).

GM tips

  • Manage expectations: players may want to kill Walkena — module explicitly redirects to diplomacy.
  • Ch 1 can sprawl — use a clock (weeks until Walkena withdraws patience) to keep favors moving.
  • Book 3 fame — reference Kiutu/Bloodsalt/Jula when Mzali officials react to PCs.
  • Osibu battle — defensive ritual is the set-piece; make Dimari-Diji's countdown visible.

Threads forward

  • Dimari-Diji → Doorway to the Red Star → **Jatembe on Akiton** (Book 5).
  • Walkena may remain antagonist in wider setting — seeds for later APs, not this book's resolution.
  • Teaching duties resume Book 5 Ch 1 until planetary alignment opens the portal.
