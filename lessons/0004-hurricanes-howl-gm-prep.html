The one-sentence pitch

Fresh **lore-speakers** teach a student cohort until **I'boko** vanishes homeward to **Kiutu**; the party pursues her through **Bloodsalt** ruins, rescues villagers from the **Knights of Abendego**, storms a cyclops **prison**, and topples **Ajbal Kimon** in **Jula** — ending at **12th level** as field teachers who model what they preach.

Chapter 1 — teaching & Bloodsalt

Opening: you are teachers now

  • Janatimo asked PCs to stay as teachers; Oyamba assigns a shared student cohort.
  • Each PC teaches their primary branch (Rain-Scribe excursions, Tempest-Sun tactics, etc.) — let players define curricula.
  • Offices often in Heron Archives; stipend covers modest housing on campus or in Nantambu.
  • Customary honorific: "Teacher" + name.

Familiar faces in the cohort

  • Ignaci — jealous but motivated; studies the PCs' "secret method."
  • Mariama — irreverent; teases slip-ups; gossip about PC mischief as students.
  • Esi Djana — now a fellow lore-speaker colleague, not a dorm peer.
  • New students: Onyiji (half-orc staff warrior), Selozè (tiefling), Jumimo (elf), I'boko (dwarf rain caller — brightest, family-first).

Early scenes (before I'boko vanishes)

  • Onyiji sparring — teachable combat review; Diplomacy/Athletics to assess tactics.
  • Student problem-of-the-week vignettes — use to establish teaching philosophy.
  • I'boko disappears when Kiutu reports Knight attacks; classmates insist on going after her.
  • Oyamba reframes departure as sanctioned research at nearby Bloodsalt ruins (Practical Research hook).

Chapters 2–4 — Sodden Lands arc

  • Knights of Abendego — long-standing threat to Kiutu; escalation drives the book.
  • Track kidnapped villagers; savanna travel with students in tow — PCs as protectors and mentors.
  • Prison of the Vacant Eye — cyclops empire remnant; don't rush — atmosphere and history matter.
  • Jula — Ajbal Kimon as BBEG; caves beneath town; rally townsfolk (social + dungeon crawl).
  • Return to Magaambya as heroes — fame sets up Book 4 Mzali delegation.

GM tips

  • Don't skip teaching prep — a session of campus life pays off when students matter in Sodden Lands.
  • Practical Research — remind players branch advancement needs discovery, not just XP.
  • Pacing: four chapters + travel; trim random encounters if Knight pursuit drags.
  • Prefab: maps/stats in PDF; your job is student relationships and moral weight of the rescue.

Threads forward

  • Heroic return → Oyamba selects PCs for **Mzali diplomacy** (Book 4).
  • Insects/egg still background; Koride on faculty roster if you want continuity.
  • Teaching semester continues in Book 5 before Akiton window opens.
