Pace estimate for Session 2

Session 1 burned most of an evening on orientation + one social-heavy Perquisite day (six players, market deep-dive). Book density ahead jumps: day 2 is a Severe-adjacent skill/combat puzzle; day 3 is a timed three-fetch + Creature 3 ambush + ritual.

BandPlan
Expected Tempest-Sun (storage barn pugwampis) and Cascade Bearers (oils + Umbo + ritual) — full days 2–3.
Stretch Open Emerald Boughs mail (Ignaci + Tzeniwe) if Cascade finishes with ≥45 minutes left. Do not force completion.
Cut early If Tempest runs long (Uduak research, bells, Anchor diplomacy, hatch talk): end after day 2 + a dorm beat. Cascade becomes Session 3 open.
Do not bank on Uzunjati storytelling or Introduction Ceremony tonight. Ceremony is a Severe 1 climax — deserves oxygen, not a rush after two full days.
Matching book pace: The module budgets ~1 week for all five Perquisite days + ceremony. Your table is slower and richer — that is fine. Keep emotional pace (service → nonlethal ethic → Uduak seed → cohort bonds) even if calendar days spill across more sessions. Do not skip NPC faces to “catch up.”

Day 2 — Tempest-Sun (pugwampi barn)

Framing

  • Esi alone at the meeting spot. Branch: defend Nantambu from threats big and small; today’s threat is small.
  • Mandate: get pugwampis out of a storage room without killing them (whack-to-head OK; lethal → they fight to kill).
  • Release off Magaambya grounds — ideally outside Nantambu.

Uduak seed load-bearing

Esi: a student was expelled for killing gremlins “because of who they are.” She knows broad strokes only; points curious PCs to teachers or records after the job. Library research on pugwampis also surfaces Uduak Basni’s story up to his presumed death.

GM only — do not say tonight

Uduak is the future Stone Ghost. This scene is the moral mirror for Cy’An’s Explode habit and the whole party’s lethal toolkit. Reward clever nonlethal / scare / Anchor Root paths with full combat XP.

Day 3 — Cascade Bearers (oils + ritual)

Framing

  • Waiting: Mariama, Anchor Root, sleeping Chizire.
  • Need oils for a ritual to help with gremlins: scarlet cap, bone, caterpillar. Eight hours or miss dinner. “Try to have fun!”
  • Anchor Root is the brief — fidgety; pep-talks make her shrink. Soft Anya / Rain-Scribe belonging beat.

Three fetches (parallelize)

OilHowWatch-outs
Bone Butcher free after 1 hour wait (1 gp to skip queue). Gather Info DC 10 → garbage heaps; crit → “lucky” talking-cow carcass (+2 craft). Carbonize + craft DC 17 Crafting/Nature (1 hour). Fail: retry; crit fail: ruin bones. Chizire auto-succeeds in 2 hours if Mariama wakes him with slugs. Grave-robbing = Magaambya accountability — shut it down.
Scarlet cap ~1 hour into jungle; climb DC 10. Umbo (weak myceloid, Creature 3) ambushes — Emit Spores / purple pox. Fleeces if <10 HP. No negotiation. Party’s weak Reflex + disease Fort DC 18. Split the tree-climber from the spore cloud.
Caterpillar Fuzzy jungle caterpillars → oil (book details in extract). Time sink; keep moving. Don’t let perfect-three-oils perfectionism eat Emerald stretch.

Ritual & aftermath

Mariama directs the ritual whether it succeeds or fails. She notes it may harm any fey, not just gremlins — Cascade caution theme. XP: up to 90 for oils + 80/120 for ritual attempt/success.

Ceremony foreshadow

Later, Kurshkin mistakes the barn clear + fey-abeyance ritual as the start of another purge — motivation for the Introduction Ceremony ambush. You don’t need to telegraph that tonight.

Stretch — Emerald mail (only if time)

  • Ignaci + Tzeniwe (elephant birds, overpacked snacks, sticky web toy from Zanvi).
  • Easy deliveries = city color + Word and Way. Hard set: illegible / descriptive addresses, lamp dispute, deific missives — each can eat real clock.
  • If you only open the day: establish faces, hand the basket, resolve 2–3 easy + one hard, then hard-cut to “dusk and ginger beer tomorrow.”

Full mail sandbox + Uzunjati + ceremony = Session 3+ material. See session log.

Steady pace rule: PC chords flavor the Perquisite; the Perquisite does not become six side quests. If a personal beat would need a scene break away from the cohort task, defer it to dorm evening or the free afternoon after Uzunjati.

Table risks & GM moves

  • Lethal AoE (Cy’An Explode, Yggostrus breath) vs nonlethal mandate — let Esi’s line land; Magaambya holds students accountable (That’s Going on Your Permanent Record sidebar).
  • Umbo is Creature 3 vs level 1 — Moderate/Severe feel. Spores punish clustered melee. Allow flee to campus for help without shame.
  • Weak party Reflex — pugwampi chaos and spore clouds both lean here; telegraph misfortune aura.
  • Time sink: perfect Kafesi-style investigation on Cascade fetches. Time-box: “dinner bell in X hours” diegetically.
  • Hatch curiosity: note + defer. “Smells wrong / goes deep” is enough.
