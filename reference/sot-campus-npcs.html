How to use this doc

  • Relationship track: Spire Dorm students move Liked → Helpful via Diplomacy, favors, or genuine friendship → grants Study/Cram bonuses (table below).
  • Voice first: Use the one-line hook to improvise; stat blocks live in the books.
  • Cohort load-bearing: Esi organizes Perquisite; Okoro runs game night; Tzeniwe bridges anadi prejudice — lean on them between missions.
  • Optional: Paizo suggests players may also roleplay a student NPC alongside their PC.

Faculty — Book 1 spotlight

Takulu Ot · LG human Cascade Bearer · Creature 4

Kindly mathematician studying how to teach. From Tunyani; sent to Magaambya as sacred ibis gift. Married to Nantambu glass artist. Office: top floor, Heron Archives. Support combat only — protects students first.

“Welcome, and a kind hello… You embark on a life of study and service.”

Koride Ulawa · CN human Rain-Scribe · Creature 6

Best entomologist in generations; loves fungus, decay, insects. Reckless with safety rules; neglects commitments. Students split — Anchor/Haibram/Chizire like her; Esi despises her. Office: Heron Archives (Black Heron building). Sends PCs to Leshy Gardens, Speaker’s Stage canal, kitchen for specimens.

“Premature conclusions are the bane of logical evaluation.”

Zuma · CN half-orc Emerald Bough

Sharp intellect + defensive magic; conspiracy rants alternate with useful rituals. Office in Elephant Museum (Golden Snake building). Tzeniwe has no patience for his “poorly shaped webs of thought.” Runs failed Tattoo Whispers ritual linking cohort verbally.

Mafika Ayuwari · Tempest-Sun celebrity

Monthly mecha-leshy jousts on Sparring Field; encourages chaos but stabilizes injured. Starday Tournament set-piece in Ch 2.

Janatimo · CG half-elf lore-speaker 12 · Book 2 lead

Takulu’s mentor. Returns two weeks after Book 1; investigates anadi arrival + griffons; promotes conversants. Simple office, curio-filled, northeast campus edge.

“Stories of the past build the events of today.”
