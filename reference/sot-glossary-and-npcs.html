Glossary

Magaambya
World's oldest magic academy; island campus in Nantambu. Founded by Old-Mage Jatembe's disciples. Service-oriented: "Word and Way."
Perquisite
Mandatory public service before becoming an attendant. Book 1 Ch 1 = five branch-themed tasks over five days.
Spirit mask / spirit face
Wooden animal mask holding a fragment of the student's spirit. First Masking ceremony (Ch 2) marks initiate → attendant.
Introduction Ceremony
End of Ch 1 — formal welcome for new initiates. Gremlins attack here; PCs hit level 2 after.
First Masking
Ch 2 ceremony in Tireless Hall; giant insects attack; PCs hit level 3 after.
Vesicant Egg
~1 ft artifact; King's psyche fragments. Koride holds it secretly. Draws insects. AP-wide MacGuffin.
King of Biting Ants
Ant-swarm sorcerer; banished to Akiton by Jatembe. Final AP villain line via the egg.
Doorway to the Red Star
Portal in Mwangi jungles; links to Akiton. Books 5–6.
Common (this AP)
Mwangi, not Taldane — per Campaign Overview.
Anadi
Shapeshifting spider-folk: human, spider, and hybrid forms. Peaceful; tight-knit communities. Book portraits often show human form — especially Tzeniwe, who usually presents human on campus. Outside the Magaambya, many Mwangi wrongly fear anadi as monsters; Nantambu/academy culture is more accepting, but reactions still vary by NPC.

Antagonists — Book 1

Stone Ghost BBEG
Uduak, expelled oread student who slaughtered gremlins during his Perquisite. Wards rebounding "killed" him; merged with campus stone. Commands gremlins; digs tunnel network.
Kurshkin secondary
Ancient pugwampi; unluck aura; grudge vs Takulu Ot. Allies with Stone Ghost. Cowardly; may survive non-lethally.
Gremlin saboteurs
Disrupt Introduction Ceremony (Ch 1). Connected to Stone Ghost's network.
