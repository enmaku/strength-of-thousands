# Strength of Thousands — Campaign Overview (GM)

Condensed from *Kindled Magic* Campaign Overview (Paizo AP #169). Spoilers throughout.

## Premise

PCs start as **initiates** at the **Magaambya** in **Nantambu**, rise to **lore-speakers** by end of Book 2, then teach for Books 3–6. The hidden through-line: **Koride Ulawa** holds the **Vesicant Egg** (fragments of the **King of Biting Ants**), which draws insects to campus until the Book 6 climax.

## Ranks

Initiate → (Perquisite) → **Attendant** (spirit mask) → **Conversant** → **Lore-speaker** (teacher)

Book 1: initiate → attendant. Book 2 ends as lore-speakers.

## Vesicant Egg & King of Biting Ants

- Jatembe banished the King through the **Doorway to the Red Star** (~−3505 AR).
- King hid psyche fragments in insects; they formed the **Vesicant Egg**.
- Koride recovered the egg before campaign start; **does not know** it connects to the King.
- Egg draws insects (and may draw anadi). Serpentfolk seek it in Book 2+.
- **Anadi:** shapeshifters (human / spider / hybrid). Tzeniwe usually presents human; Ch 1 newcomers (Goss/Mazta/Savana) arrive in hybrid — form choice = social context, not species reveal.
- Book 5: rescue Jatembe from King's airship *Mandibles of Fate* on Akiton.
- Book 6: egg hatches; PCs become **Magic Warriors**; fight colossal larva.
