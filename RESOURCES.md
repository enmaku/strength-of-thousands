# Strength of Thousands Resources

## Knowledge

- [Strength of Thousands — PathfinderWiki](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Strength_of_Thousands)
  Six-book AP summary, release dates, level ranges per volume. Use for: whole-campaign orientation.
- [Kindled Magic — PathfinderWiki](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Kindled_Magic)
  Book 1 contents list, creature/NPC index. Use for: confirming what's in the volume.
- [Magaambya — PathfinderWiki](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Magaambya)
  School history, geography, philosophy ("Word and Way"), headmaster. Use for: setting tone and Jatembe lore.
- [Strength of Thousands Player's Guide (PDF)](https://paizo.com/pathfinder/adventures/pathfinderadventurepath/strengthofthousands)
  Ranks, branches, character-building advice, spoiler-free campaign frame. Use for: sharing with players.
- [Paizo Forum: Kindled Magic GM Reference](https://paizo.com/threads/rzs43fo2?1-Kindled-Magic=)
  Spoiler-filled dev/community Q&A (Ron Lundeen answers). Use for: pacing, subsystem, author intent.
- [GM Doug's Strength of Thousands notes](https://doughahn.github.io/sot/)
  Community GM's NPC summaries and campaign timeline. Use for: NPC personality shorthand (verify against your table).
- [Paizo Blog: Strength of Thousands AP announcement](https://paizo.com/blog/strength-of-thousands-adventure-path)
  Design intent — students → teachers → world guardians. Use for: tonal compass.
- [The Mwangi Expanse (Lost Omens)](https://paizo.com/pathfinder/lostomens/worldguides/themwangiexpanse)
  Nantambu gazetteer, seasons, culture. Use for: city scenes once PCs leave campus (Book 1 mail run; essential later).

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [Paizo Forums — Strength of Thousands](https://paizo.com/community/forums/campaigns/pathfinderAdventurePath/strengthOfThousands)

- [r/Pathfinder2e](https://reddit.com/r/Pathfinder2e)
  AP megathreads and GM advice. Use for: "how did you handle academia downtime?"
