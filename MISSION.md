# Mission: GM Prep for Strength of Thousands

## Why

Run the prefab Strength of Thousands campaign for Pathfinder 2e without reading all six books cover-to-cover first. Need enough plot, character, and arc knowledge to tell the story confidently at the table — starting with Book 1 (*Kindled Magic*).

## Success looks like

- Can run any Book 1 session cold: know who matters, what's really going on, and what to foreshadow.
- Understand how Book 1 connects to the six-book arc (without spoiling players prematurely).
- Know the Magaambya's culture, ranks, branches, and recurring NPCs well enough to improvise school life.
- Have quick-reference materials to open before each session instead of re-reading the module.
- **Emphasize human themes** (service, succession, cohort, curiosity's shadow) using the thematic guide — not just plot beats.

## Constraints

- Time is limited — prioritize high-signal GM knowledge over rules minutiae (prefab handles stat blocks).
- Using the published adventure path, not homebrew.
- Teaching workspace: `/Users/enmaku/code/strength-of-thousands`; lessons should be open-and-run HTML.

## Out of scope

- Player-facing spoiler-free handouts (use Paizo's Player's Guide for that).
- Deep rules mastery of the academia subsystem (reference only; book has the DCs).
- Books 3–5 detailed lesson prep (PDF extracts available; lessons TBD).
