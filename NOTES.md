## GM context — Anadi (shapeshifters)

**Anadi have three forms:** human, spider, and hybrid (their “true” shape). Portraits in *Kindled Magic* usually show **human form** — that is not a contradiction.

- **Tzeniwe** (Spire Dorm, Emerald Boughs) is anadi; her book portrait is human-form. Her toddlers **Zachva & Zanvi** shift freely (module: spiderling on her back, human child on one knee). First Masking mask = peacock spider with crooked eyes (her kids “helped”).
- **Book 1 Ch 3:** Goss, Mazta, Savana arrive in **hybrid form** on purpose — new to Nantambu, unfamiliar with local custom (Society DC 15). Contrast with Tzeniwe, who usually passes as human and is painfully aware of spider stigma outside enlightened circles.
- **Book 2:** same three prefer spider/hybrid at Hababe Building; unsettle classmates. Tzeniwe is the natural bridge (Gather Info via her, or Diplomacy DC 17).
- **Story weight:** prejudice vs acceptance; who adapts their presentation for others vs who refuses to hide; egg may be “calling” anadi to campus (foreshadow only in Book 1).

See glossary entry + Book 1 lesson § Anadi arrival.
