# Party composition — Book 1 roster

Four imported heroes (all level 1) drive future GM teaching. Recorded from PathBuilder hero files so later lessons stay grounded in the actual table.

- **Cy'An Copperbottom** — Irongut Goblin Inventor (Construct Innovation) / Wizard Dedication (School of Unified Magical Theory), Cook. Int-key. Area attack: Explode. Cook/Bonding Meal → Lumusi Yao + cohort theme.
- **Solanum Calabash** — Fruit Leshy Magus (Twisting Tree), Astrologer. Str-key front-liner + Healing Plaster + Occultism/Oddity ID. Astrologer = Red Star (Book 5) foreshadow.
- **Taraan Skyseeker** — Skyborn Tengu **dual-class** Cleric (Warpriest of Soralyon) / Witch (The Inscribed One), Archaeologist. Wis/Int. Primary healer (Healing Font), party investigation/lore/social engine, burrowing Elemental Wisp familiar.
- **Yggostrus** — Dragonet (Homing Drake) Summoner, Dragon eidolon (acid). Cha-key. Best anti-swarm (acid breath + Caustic Blast) and party face; fragile Tiny summoner sharing HP with eidolon.

**Key cross-cutting facts (verified against AoN):**

- Universal weak save = **Reflex** (all expert Fort/Will, only trained Reflex). Book 1 insect/area attacks lean on basic Reflex.
- Swarms have **weakness to area/splash**, resist physical, immune to precision → only Yggostrus and Cy'An have real anti-swarm AoE; melee crew is resisted.
- **Soralyon** = guardians/monuments/magic (NOT masks) — maps onto guarding the Magaambya and the rune-covered oread Stone Ghost. Taraan is the natural moral center of the Ch 3 finale.
- Party is healing-rich (Font + Healing Plaster + Fruit Leshy fruit).
- Non-lethal Perquisite mandate clashes with the party's lethal AoE — intentional Uduak mirror.
